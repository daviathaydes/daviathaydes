## Hi there, I'm Davi Athaydes Leite 👋

I'm a Master's student in Political Science at the [Institute of Social and Political Studies (IESP-UERJ)](https://iesp.uerj.br/), with a strong interest in quantitative methods, working at the intersection of data science and social science research. Also, I hold a Bachelor's degree in Public Administration from [Universidade Estadual do Norte Fluminense (UENF)](https://uenf.br/portal/).

I am also affiliated with research groups such as the Núcleo de Estudos em Representação e Democracia (NERD) and the [Laboratory for Monitoring and Evaluation of Policies and Elections (MAPE)](https://mape.org.br/).

I am interested in politics at the municipal level, especially the dynamics of local power — such as legislative majorities, reelection, and the functioning of city councils. I also study the relationship between municipalities and the federal government, mainly in the distribution of discretionary resources and the political factors that influence this. Methodologically, I work with a quantitative approach, using observational data and causal inference.
